Skyra is a techno project of Sandro Papaskiri - Georgian Dj and Producer. The young artist first appeared on Tbilisi scene two years ago and has since been actively playing in clubs such as : KHIDI, Mtkvarze, Cafe Gallery and more.

Skyra's work was greatly influenced by the development of the local techno scene, which helped him to find his own sound. His music mostly consists of experimental and ambient voices.

Skyra's first release in collaboration with three other georgian artists came out in 2016 on the georgian label "Icontrax" in the collection of "Lifesigns 001", in addition to his another techno Project YT. His second work on the same label was released in the following year alongside the Georgian artists Newa and Body thrills, in the collection « Icontrax Two ».
